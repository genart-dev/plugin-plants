## Key Architecture Notes for Grammar Authoring

### Geometric Models
- **Water lily pad**: Circle with V-notch (sinus), radial veins from center, flat on water
- **Lotus pad**: Complete circle (no notch), peltate (petiole attaches to center underneath), raised above water
- **Sea lettuce**: Irregular perimeter generated by noise-distorted circle, ruffled edge

### L-system Models
- **Giant kelp**: Linear stipe with alternate blades, holdfast = inverted branching tree
- **Gas bladders**: Spherical at blade base, provide buoyancy

### Phyllotaxis Models
- **Duckweed**: Budding from pocket at frond edge, 2-3 daughter fronds per parent
